2. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information to provide you with a personalized travel experience and for market analysis purposes. The data we collect includes:

2.1. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Registration Data: When you register via email, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Social Media Data: If you register with Facebook, we collect your full name, email address, and profile picture, according to the permissions you grant us.
  • Profile and Onboarding Data: Name, surname, country of origin, age, gender, travel style (solo, couple, etc.), travel budget (budget, mid-range, luxury), your trip dates, and your tourist interests or preferences (beaches, culture, etc.).

2.2. Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our application, such as the features you use or the places you save. (Currently not implemented, but planned).
  • Device Data: Basic information about your mobile device, such as the model and operating system, to ensure compatibility and improve the app.

3. How and Why We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To Operate and Improve the App: To personalize your experience, create itineraries, and offer relevant recommendations.
  • For Communication: To send you important information about your account or changes to our services.
  • For Big Data Analysis (Primary Purpose): We aggregate and anonymize demographic and preference data (country, age, interests, budget) to generate statistical reports. These reports are shared with local businesses in Malta (hotels, restaurants, tour operators) to help them understand tourism trends and improve their services. We never share your name, email, or direct contact information.

4. Who Do We Share Your Information With?

Your trust is essential. We only share your data under the following circumstances:

  • With Local Partner Companies: We share statistical reports and aggregated, anonymized data. For example, a report might state that "30% of tourists aged 25-35 from Spain are interested in diving."
  • For Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to competent authorities.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident, you have the following rights over your data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data from the "Edit Profile" screen.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request the deletion of your account and all your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that we provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time, which will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [Your Support or Privacy Email].

6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use the secure infrastructure of Supabase to store and protect your information.
